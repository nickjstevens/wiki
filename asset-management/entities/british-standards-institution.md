# British Standards Institution

## Overview
The British Standards Institution (BSI) appears in this source set as the UK standards body publishing the British Standard implementations of [[iso-55000]], [[iso-55001]], and [[iso-55002]], while also co-publishing the earlier [[pas-55]] specification with strong IAM involvement.

## Role in the discipline
These documents show BSI as a bridge between international standardization and UK practice. The national forewords place the ISO asset-management standards into a UK adoption context, while PAS 55 shows BSI's earlier role in formalizing physical-asset-management practice before the ISO 55000 series.

## Why it matters
For this wiki, BSI is an institutional anchor for how infrastructure asset-management concepts move from guidance into auditable, widely referenced requirements and application guidance.
