# Office for Nuclear Regulation

## Overview
The Office for Nuclear Regulation (ONR) appears here as the UK nuclear regulator setting expectations for how duty-holders manage whole-of-life risks from physical assets on licensed sites.

## Role in the discipline
Its asset-management technical assessment guide ties asset management to licence compliance, safety assessment, preservation planning, proactive replacement, configuration management, obsolescence, and supply-chain considerations.

## Why it matters
ONR shows how general asset-management ideas become regulatory expectations in a high-consequence sector. It strengthens the practical and assurance-focused side of [[nuclear-asset-management]], [[asset-management-system]], and [[risk-management]].
