# Global Forum on Maintenance and Asset Management

## Overview
The Global Forum on Maintenance and Asset Management (GFMAM) appears here as a collaborative international body that aligns maintenance and asset-management knowledge across member organizations.

## Role in the discipline
Across the Landscape, maturity position statement, and digital-transformation publication, GFMAM acts less like a regulator and more like a consensus builder. It organizes the field, publishes shared frameworks, and connects asset management to practical capability development beyond minimum conformance.

## Why it matters
GFMAM broadens the wiki beyond one institution or one standard family. It links [[asset-management-maturity]], [[digital-transformation-in-asset-management]], and the broader field structure reflected in [[iam-10-box-capabilities-model]].
