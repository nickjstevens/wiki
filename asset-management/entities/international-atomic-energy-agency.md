# International Atomic Energy Agency

## Overview
The International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA) appears in this source set as a major publisher of nuclear-sector guidance linking safety, operations, risk, and long-horizon asset stewardship.

## Role in the discipline
The IAEA documents here emphasize that asset management in nuclear settings is not just about equipment condition. It is about informed choices over financial performance, operational performance, risk, maintenance strategy, and long-term plant sustainability.

## Why it matters
In this wiki, the IAEA is a key institutional source for [[nuclear-asset-management]], [[reliability-centered-maintenance]], and the risk-informed extension of [[asset-management-decision-making]].
