# Institute of Asset Management

## Overview
The Institute of Asset Management (IAM) appears in this source set as a central professional body advancing infrastructure asset-management practice through guidance, capability models, maturity frameworks, and competence development.

## Role in the discipline
In this PDF, IAM acts as both explainer and organizer of the field. It provides a structured language for [[asset-management]], clarifies the relationship between the broader discipline and the [[asset-management-system]], and promotes the [[iam-10-box-capabilities-model]] as a practical organizing framework.

## Why it matters
IAM helps turn broad ideas such as value, lifecycle thinking, governance, and risk into an actionable discipline. In this sense it is an institutional anchor for later pages on [[asset-management-decision-making]] and [[value-realization]].
