## Concepts

- [[asset-information]] — Information foundation for understanding assets, uncertainty, condition, and decision consequences across the lifecycle.
- [[asset-management]] — Organization-wide discipline for realizing value from assets through aligned, whole-of-life, outcomes-focused decision-making.
- [[asset-management-decision-making]] — Structured, value-based process for choosing among asset options under uncertainty across the full life cycle.
- [[asset-management-maturity]] — Capability-development view showing that value delivery extends beyond bare ISO 55001 conformance.
- [[asset-management-system]] — Formal management-system layer for asset management, aligned with ISO 55001 but narrower than the overall discipline.
- [[asset-review]] — Review and improvement discipline connecting assurance, learning, and performance outcomes.
- [[digital-transformation-in-asset-management]] — Use of data, analytics, and digital systems to improve maintenance and asset-management outcomes.
- [[iam-10-box-capabilities-model]] — IAM's updated capability model for organizing the full asset-management discipline.
- [[iso-55000]] — Vocabulary, principles, outcomes, and maturity-oriented overview of the ISO 55000 asset-management family.
- [[iso-55001]] — Requirements standard for an asset management system, including planning, decision-making, and improvement expectations.
- [[iso-55002]] — Application guidance for implementing ISO 55001 in practice.
- [[lifecycle-delivery]] — Translation of strategy into acquisition, operation, maintenance, renewal, and end-of-life actions.
- [[line-of-sight]] — Alignment principle connecting organizational purpose and objectives to asset-management decisions, plans, and delivery.
- [[nuclear-asset-management]] — Asset-management application in nuclear settings where safety, compliance, long life, and assurance are tightly coupled.
- [[pas-55]] — Pre-ISO specification for the optimized management of physical assets and a key historical precursor to the ISO series.
- [[physical-asset-management]] — Strategic whole-life management of fixed or engineering assets, narrower and more concrete than the broader asset-management discipline.
- [[reliability-centered-maintenance]] — Structured approach for designing maintenance around functions, failure consequences, and effective interventions.
- [[risk-management]] — Systematic treatment of uncertainty in asset decisions, linking prioritization, treatment, tolerance, and monitoring.
- [[value-realization]] — Core aim of turning assets into desired financial and non-financial outcomes over appropriate time horizons.
