# Lifecycle Delivery

## Overview
[[lifecycle-delivery]] covers how asset strategies are translated into acquisition, operation, maintenance, renewal, replacement, and end-of-life actions.

## What the sources emphasize
The core-practices paper treats lifecycle delivery as one of the validated dimensions of physical asset management. The whole-life and project-delivery sources reinforce that choices made early in design, governance, procurement, and delivery shape later operating cost, risk, and performance.

## Why it matters
This is where long-range intent becomes real interventions and projects. Good lifecycle delivery depends on sound [[asset-management-decision-making]], clear [[line-of-sight]], and realistic treatment of risk and delivery constraints.
