# Reliability-Centered Maintenance

## Overview
[[reliability-centered-maintenance]] (RCM) is a structured method for designing maintenance programmes around functions, failure consequences, and the most effective tasks.

## What the source emphasizes
The IAEA TECDOC presents RCM as a way to eliminate unnecessary maintenance, address deficiencies in maintenance programmes, and improve operator-maintainer interaction while reducing pressure on limited maintenance resources.

## Why it matters
RCM is a concrete example of how [[risk-management]] and [[asset-management-decision-making]] shape lifecycle choices instead of defaulting to routine maintenance intervals.
