# ISO 55000

## Overview
[[iso-55000]] is the vocabulary, overview, and principles document of the ISO 55000 series. In the 2024 edition, it explicitly frames asset management through principles, outcomes and benefits, the relationship between asset management, the [[asset-management-system]], and assets, and indicators of [[asset-management-maturity]].

## What it contributes
This document gives the conceptual frame rather than the auditable requirements. It clarifies what the discipline is for, what outcomes it seeks, and how to distinguish the wider discipline of [[asset-management]] from the system used to govern it.

## Why it matters
For this wiki, ISO 55000 is the cleanest standards-based entry point into the field's language and structure.
