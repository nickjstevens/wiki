# Digital Transformation in Asset Management

## Overview
[[digital-transformation-in-asset-management]] refers to the use of data, analytics, digital systems, and related organizational changes to improve maintenance and asset-management outcomes across the lifecycle.

## What the sources emphasize
The GFMAM publication frames digital transformation in terms of implications across the lifecycle and across the asset-management landscape, while the big-data architecture paper argues that analytics should support equipment health, reliability, visualization, and better decisions rather than just collecting more data.

## Practical meaning
In this wiki, digital transformation matters when it strengthens [[asset-information]], improves [[asset-management-decision-making]], and supports better maintenance and reliability performance.
