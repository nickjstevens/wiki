# Risk Management

## Definition
Risk management in this domain is the systematic treatment of uncertainty affecting organizational objectives, asset performance, service outcomes, and compliance.

## Why it matters
Risk is foundational to prioritization, intervention timing, and resource allocation. It connects directly to [[asset-management-decision-making]] and shapes how organizations interpret criticality, resilience, and acceptable consequence.

## Practical use
The source supports a structured approach that includes identifying, assessing, prioritizing, treating, and monitoring risk. It also implies that risk should be understood in relation to [[value-realization]] rather than as an isolated compliance exercise.
