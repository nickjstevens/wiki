# ISO 55002

## Overview
[[iso-55002]] provides application guidance for implementing [[iso-55001]]. Its contents emphasize scope definition, actions for risks and opportunities, planning to achieve asset-management objectives, and the investigation and improvement of both management-system and asset-management nonconformities.

## What it contributes
This document is implementation-oriented. It helps organizations move from requirement statements toward practical design and operation of the [[asset-management-system]].

## Why it matters
It is especially useful where the standard itself is too terse and the organization needs clearer guidance on review, continual improvement, and how planning and support mechanisms should work together.
