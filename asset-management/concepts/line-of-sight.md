# Line of Sight

## Definition
Line of sight is the desirable alignment through an organization such that work at every level contributes demonstrably to agreed objectives.

## Why it matters
In infrastructure asset management, line of sight prevents strategy, planning, delivery, and assurance from becoming disconnected silos. It connects organization purpose to asset-management objectives, plans, and day-to-day choices.

## Practical role
This concept is central to translating policy into action and to ensuring that [[asset-management-decision-making]] and [[asset-management-system]] design remain connected to outcomes rather than becoming paperwork exercises.
