# ISO 55001

## Overview
[[iso-55001]] is the requirements standard for an [[asset-management-system]]. The 2024 edition highlights clearer treatment of asset-management decision-making, the strategic asset management plan, and the distinction between addressing risk and addressing opportunity.

## What it contributes
Where [[iso-55000]] explains the discipline, ISO 55001 defines what a conforming management system shall include. It turns high-level intent into requirements for leadership, planning, support, operation, evaluation, and improvement.

## Why it matters
It is the main standards reference for translating [[asset-management]] into auditable organizational arrangements without claiming to exhaust the discipline.
