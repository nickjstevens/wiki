# Asset Review

## Overview
[[asset-review]] is the discipline of checking whether asset-management arrangements and outcomes remain effective, aligned, and improvable.

## What the sources emphasize
The core-practices paper treats asset review as a core latent variable linked to operational performance. ISO 55002 and PAS 55 reinforce the same theme through investigation of nonconformities, management review, and continual improvement.

## Why it matters
Review closes the loop between plans and results. It is the practical mechanism linking assurance, learning, and [[asset-management-maturity]].
