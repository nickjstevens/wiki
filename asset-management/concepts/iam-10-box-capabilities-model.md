# IAM 10-box Capabilities Model

## Overview
The IAM 10-box capabilities model is the source's preferred framework for organizing the full asset-management discipline beyond minimum management-system conformance.

## Purpose
The model gives a practical structure for viewing the field as an interconnected set of capabilities, from context and governance through delivery, information, risk, review, and outcomes.

## Why it matters
It is useful as a navigation backbone for this wiki because it shows how [[asset-management]], [[asset-management-system]], [[risk-management]], and [[value-realization]] fit together.

## Ten capabilities at a glance
- purpose and context
- leadership and governance
- organization and people
- strategy and planning
- asset management decision-making
- life cycle delivery
- information management
- risk
- review and continual improvement
- value and outcomes
