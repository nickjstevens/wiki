# PAS 55

## Overview
[[pas-55]] is the earlier Publicly Available Specification for the optimized management of physical assets. Its structure covers asset-management policy, strategy, objectives and plans, enablers and controls, implementation, performance assessment, and management review.

## What it contributes
The source shows PAS 55 as a direct predecessor to later ISO work, with a strong physical-asset orientation and explicit practical structure for coordinated lifecycle management.

## Why it matters
It remains useful for understanding the historical development of the field and the transition from [[physical-asset-management]] practice into the broader ISO 55000 family.
