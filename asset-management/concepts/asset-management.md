# Asset Management

## Definition
The IAM Anatomy defines asset management as the coordinated activity of an organization to realize value from assets.

## Core meaning in this wiki
The emphasis is not just on maintaining physical things. Asset management is presented as a whole-organization discipline linking purpose, governance, planning, delivery, information, risk, and performance across the life of assets and asset systems.

## Key principles
The source emphasizes an outcomes focus, responsiveness to organizational context, alignment across the organization, a whole-of-life perspective, and the importance of culture and leadership.
