# Asset Information

## Overview
[[asset-information]] is the structured information base used to understand assets, their context, their condition, and the consequences of decisions.

## What the sources emphasize
The core-practices paper identifies asset information as a central practice area. The big-data and digital-transformation sources add that information quality matters more than data volume: analysis, visualization, and the connection between maintenance staff, analysts, and decision-makers are what make information useful.

## Why it matters
Without credible information, organizations cannot prioritize, review outcomes, or make risk-aware choices. Asset information therefore underpins [[asset-management-decision-making]], [[asset-review]], and digital enablement.
