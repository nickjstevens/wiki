# Nuclear Asset Management

## Overview
[[nuclear-asset-management]] applies asset-management principles in a high-consequence environment where safety, regulatory compliance, long asset lives, and operational continuity all matter at once.

## What the sources emphasize
The IAEA publication frames asset management as informed decision-making that balances financial performance, operational performance, and risk for sustainable plant operation. The EPRI toolkit adds a practical industry view of nuclear asset-management analysis and support tools. INSAG-25 reinforces integrated risk-informed decisions, and ONR adds expectations around whole-of-life risk, preservation, replacement, obsolescence, configuration management, and supply chain.

## Why it matters
This concept shows how generic asset-management ideas become more explicit, conservative, and assurance-heavy in nuclear settings.
