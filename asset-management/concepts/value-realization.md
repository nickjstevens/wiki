# Value Realization

## Definition
Value realization is the central aim of asset management: turning assets and asset systems into desired financial and non-financial outcomes over time.

## Why it matters
The IAM source makes clear that value is broader than short-term cost or accounting value. It includes outcomes, impacts, stakeholder effects, service quality, resilience, and long-term whole-of-life performance.

## Relationship to decisions
This concept ties together [[asset-management]], [[asset-management-decision-making]], and [[risk-management]]. Without an explicit value frame, optimization and intervention planning lose their purpose.
