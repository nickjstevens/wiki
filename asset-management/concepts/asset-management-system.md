# Asset Management System

## Definition
An asset management system is the structured set of policies, objectives, processes, and controls used to direct and coordinate asset-management activity.

## Important distinction
The PDF is explicit that the broader discipline of [[asset-management]] is larger than the management system for asset management. ISO 55001 describes requirements for the system, but not the full scope of practice or all implementation detail.

## Practical meaning
A good asset management system should align context, leadership, planning, support, operational control, performance evaluation, and improvement. It should strengthen rather than substitute for [[line-of-sight]] and [[asset-management-decision-making]].
