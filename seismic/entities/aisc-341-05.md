# AISC 341-05 with Supplement No. 1

AISC 341-05 is the seismic provisions standard for structural steel buildings. In this ingest batch it anchors code-level requirements for ductile steel seismic systems, expected inelastic mechanisms, and detailing-compatible design assumptions.

## Why it matters

- It defines the steel-system side of capacity design and ductile detailing.
- It should be read together with connection qualification material such as [[aisc-358-05]].
- It provides the code context for [[seismic-design-of-steel-moment-frames]].

## Evidence from this ingest batch

- The ingested PDF is the 2005 standard with Supplement No. 1 and supersedes older AISC seismic provisions.
