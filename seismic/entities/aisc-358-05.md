# AISC 358-05

AISC 358-05 provides prequalified moment-frame connections for seismic applications. In this wiki it is the connection-detailing companion to [[aisc-341-05]], especially for ensuring that intended ductile mechanisms can actually be achieved in steel moment frames.

## Why it matters

- Connection behavior is often the difference between nominal code compliance and reliable inelastic performance.
- Prequalification narrows uncertainty in analytical idealization and expected plastic hinge formation.
- The standard directly supports [[seismic-design-of-steel-moment-frames]].

## Evidence from this ingest batch

- The ingested PDF identifies the standard as covering prequalified connections for special and intermediate steel moment frames.
