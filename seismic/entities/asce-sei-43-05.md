# ASCE/SEI 43-05

ASCE/SEI 43-05 is the seismic design criteria standard for structures, systems, and components in nuclear facilities. Within this wiki it represents the design-criteria counterpart to the analysis-focused [[asce-sei-4-16]].

## Why it matters

- It frames target performance and design expectations for nuclear facilities.
- It is a key standards bridge between hazard characterization, structural analysis, and seismic qualification.
- It gives nuclear work a different baseline from ordinary building-code design provisions.
