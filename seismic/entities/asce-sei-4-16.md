# ASCE/SEI 4-16

ASCE/SEI 4-16 is the ASCE standard focused on seismic analysis of safety-related nuclear structures. In this ingest batch it functions as a core reference point for how nuclear facilities connect hazard definition, structural modeling, damping choices, floor spectra generation, and qualification workflows.

## Why it matters

- It anchors the analysis side of nuclear seismic practice rather than general building-code minimum design.
- It provides the standards context for pages on [[nuclear-facility-seismic-analysis-and-design-criteria]], [[modal-combination-methods-and-floor-response-spectra]], and [[rayleigh-damping-in-seismic-analysis]].
- It helps distinguish nuclear qualification workflows from ordinary building design approximations.
