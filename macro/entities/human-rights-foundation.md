# Human Rights Foundation

## Overview
The Human Rights Foundation appears in this source set as an institution linking human-rights advocacy with Bitcoin, privacy, open-source communications, anti-CBDC monitoring, and anti-authoritarian technology programs.

## Why it matters in this wiki
HRF treats Bitcoin less as a speculative asset and more as practical infrastructure for people exposed to financial repression. Its programs make the connection between [[bitcoin]] and [[financial-freedom-under-authoritarianism]] concrete rather than merely rhetorical.

## Programs highlighted in the report
- Financial Freedom Report and related education
- Bitcoin Development Fund
- CBDC Tracker
- global events, summits, and nonprofit training
- AI for Individual Rights

## Strategic relevance
HRF's reporting also intersects with [[bitcoin-quantum-risk]], because the report frames quantum security as part of protecting freedom-preserving financial infrastructure.
