# Cycle Trading Patterns

## Definition
Cycle trading patterns refers here to the Bressert-style trading approach built around recurring bar-count cycles, detrending, oscillators, trend filters, and timing bands.

## Core method
The manual emphasizes a dominant cycle often clustering around roughly 20 bars, with centered detrending used for historical identification and oscillator-style proxies used in real time. It also stresses that the higher-timeframe trend should dominate lower-timeframe entries.

## Translation and timing
The framework distinguishes right translation in bullish conditions from left translation in bearish conditions, and uses timing bands to estimate likely turning windows. These are presented as practical trading tools, not deep macro laws.
